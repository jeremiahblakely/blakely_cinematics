FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about your cinematic portrait session

Sessions start at $450 for our signature cinematic portrait package. We offer several packages depending on your needs - from professional headshots to full creative sessions.

Visit our Services & Pricing page for detailed package information and current rates.

Currently, bookings are handled through Instagram @blakely_cinematics or email. We're launching an online scheduling system soon that will show real-time availability.

We typically book sessions 4-6 weeks in advance, so reach out early to secure your preferred date.

We require a $150 deposit to secure your session date. This deposit is applied to your session total.

The deposit is refundable if cancelled more than 7 days before your session. Cancellations within 7 days forfeit the deposit.

The remaining balance is due on the day of your session. We accept:

  • Cash (preferred - no fees)
  • Venmo/Zelle (no fees)
  • Credit/debit cards (2.75% processing fee)

For Headshots (Theatrical/Commercial):

  • Bring 3-4 outfit options with varying necklines (v-neck, crew neck, scoop)
  • Solid colors work best - avoid busy patterns or large logos
  • NO accessories - they distract from your face
  • Clothes should be wrinkle-free and clean

For Creative/Cinematic Portraits:

  • More artistic freedom with styling and accessories
  • Layers and textures enhance the cinematic look

Hair & Makeup: Arrive camera-ready or book one of our recommended makeup artists. Clean skin and styled hair are essential.

We'll send you a detailed preparation guide once you book!

For a comprehensive guide, view our detailed Preparation Guide.

Sessions take place at our private studio in the Orange County/Brea area. The exact address and parking instructions will be provided in your booking confirmation email.

We also offer on-location shoots at your preferred location for an additional travel fee.

Your edited images will be delivered via password-protected online gallery within 10-14 business days after your session.

You'll receive high-resolution files for download, and the gallery remains active for 90 days. Images are optimized for both print and web use, perfect for casting submissions and social media.

All images include professional color grading and basic retouching to maintain a natural, cinematic look. This includes:

  • Color correction and cinematic grading
  • Basic blemish removal
  • Exposure and contrast adjustments

Advanced retouching services are available through our partner retouchers for an additional fee if you need more extensive editing.

Life happens! We understand that plans can change.

You may reschedule once with at least 48 hours notice at no charge. Additional reschedules or those within 48 hours incur a $75 fee.

For cancellations: Deposits are refundable if cancelled more than 7 days before your session. Cancellations within 7 days forfeit the deposit.

Weather: For outdoor sessions, weather-related reschedules are always free.

While we don't provide hair and makeup services directly, we maintain a list of recommended professional makeup artists and hair stylists on our Recommended Vendors page.

We suggest booking makeup artists at least 2 weeks in advance of your session. Most charge between $150-250 for headshot makeup.

If you're doing your own makeup, we recommend:

  • Slightly heavier application than daily wear (for camera)
  • Matte products to reduce shine
  • Well-defined eyes and brows
  • Bring powder and lipstick for touch-ups
