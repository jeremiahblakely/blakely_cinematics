RECOMMENDED VENDORS

Trusted professionals we recommend to help you look your absolute best for your session

Before Booking

These talented professionals are independent vendors we've worked with and recommend. When booking, please:

  • Book at least 2 weeks in advance for availability
  • Mention you're shooting with Blakely Cinematics
  • Discuss your session goals and look preferences
  • Confirm rates and travel fees if applicable
  • Allow 60-90 minutes for makeup, 30-60 for hair styling
